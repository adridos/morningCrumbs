Our Story

A little about
Morning Crumbs

Morning Crumbs became official in April 2026, but the story really started in the spring of 2025, when my little guy was just learning to crawl. Like a lot of new moms, I loved being home with him — and I also needed something of my own to pour into. That’s when my mom introduced me to sourdough. She handed me a jar of her starter, taught me her rhythms, and I was hooked. There was something about the slowness of it — the long rise, the patient shaping — that I could do with my baby in my arms.

What started as one quiet hobby has grown into something I never expected. I find so much joy in the act of baking — and just as much in hearing the way others enjoy my breads. Thank you for being part of it. I’m so happy to bake for you and your family. And thank you to my favorite crumb makers, Kyle & Louis.

— Adriana
